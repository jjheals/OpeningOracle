
A gambit answer to the Ruy Lopez - Black's move 3...f5 strikes the white center from the side, leading to great complications in many cases.
Pros: 

Black tries to grab the initiative
Black can often gain a strong pawn center
The f-file can be used by a black rook.

Cons:

Black can end up down one or two pawns
The black king is exposed
There are many forcing variations
